Sherlock

Sherlock Is a modernized twist on Arthur Conan Doyle characters. The show produced and ran a total of 4 seasons over the course of 6 years. The show focused primarily on Sherlock Holmes and his partner Dr. John Watson solving detective work around London and Holmes' nemesis Jim Moriarty
