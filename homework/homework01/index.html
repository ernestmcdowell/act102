Da Nang Vietnam

Culture

Da Nang Vietnam is the fifth largest city in Vietnam by population. It is also the the cultural and educational hub for all of central Vietnam. Tourism is a major component in Da Nang's economy, attracting millions of people every year. Sites include, The Dragon Bridge, the Bà Nà Mountains, and the Hands of God.

History

Ancient Da Nang

The history of Da Nang dates back to 192 AD to the Champa kingdom. Da Nang has been heavily contested throughout history. land disputes between ancient civilizations in the area date back over several centuries.

Vietnam War

During the Vietnam War, Da Nang international airport was used a base of operations for the South Vietnamese and US Air Force and was the most active airport in the world. On an average day During the war the airport would handle over 2500 air operations.

Economy

The currency in Vietnam is the Vietnam dong. $1.00 USD is equivalent to around $22,500 VND. Da Nang's primary economy drivers are Agriculture, forestry, trade, and tourism. From tourism alone Da Nang generated over $850 million USD (19.4 trillion VND) in 2017.
