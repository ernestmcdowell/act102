2001: A Space Odyssey

2001: A Space Odyssey is movie about a group of astronauts aboard a spacecraft called Discovery One. The astronauts and their highly sophisticated computer intelligence, HAL 9000 are on their way to Jupiter. HAL claims that he is not capable of making errors. Throughout the movie HAL and two astronauts, Frank Poole, and Dr. David Bowman go about carrying on their daily routines as usual while the rest of the crew is in hybernation. HAL eventually starts to view the astronauts as a source of all problems. Leading to a contentious and dangerous journey through space.
